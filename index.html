omnom.tech

This innovative robot developed for the entrepreneur project at our school utilizes numerous sensors and motors to create the user a breakfast. It connects with your phone through an app (supports iOS and Android) and it can also connect through a website and Amazon Alexa. The platform is fully Internet connected and allows for the integration of the robot to numerous different services. Currently, the robot only makes toast and bacon but we plan to expand the food making capabilities to pancakes, eggs, and more.
