Airtar

Air guitar irl

At HackingEDU, we (Jesse Liang, Nathan Lo, Derrick Ng, Giang Duong, and me) created Airtar in 36 hours. Using XCode7, C++, the Leapmotion, two Myos and the hardware's SDKs, we managed to develop a pretty accurate air guitar. The code is open sourced on Github.

  • Date: October 23 - 25, 2015

Laser Harp

Arduinos, lasers, music

The Laser Harp is a simple Arduino based piece of hardware with the ability to shoot lasers and create music using the laser "strings". It was created using Arduino C, knowledge of circuits, and knowledge of microcontrollers.
